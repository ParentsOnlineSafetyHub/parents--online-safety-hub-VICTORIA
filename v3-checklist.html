What this covers

  • Quick setup: privacy, messaging, voice chat, and location settings
  • Family rules: boundaries, expectations, and safer behaviour
  • Warning signs: red flags that need attention
  • What to do next: evidence preservation and reporting steps
This checklist is built to make regular online safety conversations easier, clearer, and more consistent for families.

Best way to use it

Use this checklist as a repeatable rhythm, not a one-off reaction. A short check done regularly is more useful than one big panic check after something feels wrong.

Weekly: privacy settings, new apps, friend lists, chat exposure

Monthly: device controls, house rules, age fit, warning signs

Any time concern rises: move into First 24 Hours, Real Scenarios, or Evidence & Reporting
