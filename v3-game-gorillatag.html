Gorilla Tag

One of the most popular VR games for kids — and fully voice based.

Why Gorilla Tag matters

Gorilla Tag has open voice chat where children interact freely with strangers.

Parents often assume it is safe because it looks simple.

Voice chat with strangers happens constantly

Safety precautions

Monitor play sessions

Disable voice chat where possible

Limit friend requests

Review who your child plays with

Red flags

  • Older players befriending kids
  • Requests to move to Discord
  • Private room invitations
  • Requests for personal information
