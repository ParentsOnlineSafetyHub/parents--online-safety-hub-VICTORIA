Parent Approval for Digital Gifting
A safer standard for child accounts in Australia.

This is a practical safety change.
Not a ban. Not overreach.
Just one extra step that stops a known risk early.

The petition question

Should Australian laws require parental approval before children can receive digital gifts, in-game currency, skins, or online items from others?

This targets a known grooming entry point — early trust-building through gifting

Why this matters in real life

  • Predators often use gifts to build trust with children
  • “Free Robux”, skins, or items create emotional connection
  • This can lead to private chats and secrecy
  • Parents often don’t know it’s happening early
This is not about the gift.
It’s about what the gift is used to start.
