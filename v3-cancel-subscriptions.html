Cancel Subscriptions

Stop money leaks. Cancel recurring payments across your accounts.

Why this matters

Subscriptions quietly stack up. Cancelling a few can immediately free up money for bills, food, and family stability. Check every account you’ve ever used.

Start here (most common)

Google Play Apple App Store Microsoft

Google Play (Android)

1) Open Play Store

2) Tap your profile icon (top right)

3) Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions

4) Tap each subscription → Cancel

5) Repeat for every Google account you’ve used

Download Google Guide (PDF)

Apple App Store (iPhone / iPad)

1) Settings → Your Name (Apple ID)

2) Subscriptions

3) Select subscription → Cancel

4) Check any other Apple IDs used on the device

Download Apple Guide (PDF)

Microsoft

1) Sign in to your Microsoft account

2) Services & subscriptions

3) Manage → Cancel or turn off recurring billing

4) Check Xbox + Microsoft 365 + OneDrive

Download Microsoft Guide (PDF)
