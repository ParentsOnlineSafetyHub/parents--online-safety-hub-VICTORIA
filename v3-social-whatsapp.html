Priority risks

WhatsApp is mostly phone-number based. Group invites and unknown numbers are the main risks.

Step-by-step privacy settings

1) Privacy controls
WhatsApp → Settings → Privacy.
Set:
• Last Seen/Online: My Contacts (or Nobody)
• Profile Photo: My Contacts
• About: My Contacts
• Status: My Contacts

2) Groups
Privacy → Groups → set to My Contacts.
This stops random additions.

3) Block unknown numbers
Teach: unknown number → do not reply → block.

4) Two-step verification
Settings → Account → Two-step verification → ON.
