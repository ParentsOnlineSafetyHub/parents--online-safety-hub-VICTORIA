Priority risks

YouTube can expose kids via autoplay, shorts, comments, DMs (via other apps), and algorithm suggestions. Use YouTube Kids for younger children.

Step-by-step safety settings

1) Restricted Mode
YouTube → Settings → General → Restricted Mode ON.

2) Turn off Autoplay
On the video player, toggle Autoplay OFF.

3) Manage Search & Watch History
Settings → History & privacy → pause watch/search history (reduces algorithm “rabbit holes”).

4) Comments rule
Teach kids: never reply to strangers in comments. Screenshot/report anything sexual, threatening, or manipulative.

5) Use Family controls
Use Family Link (Android) / Screen Time (Apple) to restrict access times.
