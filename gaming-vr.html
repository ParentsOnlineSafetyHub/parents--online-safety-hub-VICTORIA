VR — Core Safety Checklist

Main risks: immersive voice chat, adult public rooms, harassment, private worlds.

Immediate Protection

  • ☐ Treat VR as a higher-risk environment if there is voice chat
  • ☐ Restrict friend requests
  • ☐ Avoid public rooms for younger users
  • ☐ No private sessions with strangers
  • ☐ Strong supervision recommended
