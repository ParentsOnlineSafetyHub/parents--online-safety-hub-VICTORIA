Platforms

Lock the platform first. Then lock the game or app running on it.

Why platforms matter

Platforms control communication, friend requests, purchases, invites, and multiplayer access.

These settings are often stronger than the settings inside the game itself.

Platform first. Then app and game safety.

Choose the platform

PC PlayStation Xbox Nintendo Switch Virtual Reality

What to lock on every platform

1) Messages and private communication

2) Friend requests and invites

3) Voice chat and multiplayer exposure

4) Purchases and spending

5) Who can join or contact the child
