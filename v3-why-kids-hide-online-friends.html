Why Kids Hide Online Friends

“It’s just a friend” can still matter.
The question is not only whether they have online friends — it is why they feel they need to hide them.

The important question

Who are they talking to?

Where did the friendship start?

Has it moved private?

Are they being asked to keep it secret?

Hidden online friendships can become risk when secrecy or pressure appears.

Why kids hide online friends

  • They think parents will overreact
  • They want privacy
  • They feel emotionally attached
  • The other person told them not to tell
  • They are embarrassed
  • They do not recognise the risk

When it becomes concerning

  • The friend is older or unknown
  • The conversation moved to another app
  • The child becomes defensive
  • The person gives gifts or attention
  • The child is told to keep secrets
  • The friendship affects mood or behaviour

What parents can say

“I’m not against online friends.”
