Roblox

One of the most popular games for children — and one of the most commonly used for grooming contact.

Why Roblox matters

Roblox is not just a game. It is a massive social platform with millions of player-created worlds.

Children can interact with strangers through chat, private servers, friend systems, and shared game spaces.

Most grooming cases begin with casual conversations inside games
Child Safety First: Roblox can look playful and harmless, but it still creates access to strangers, private contact, and off-platform movement.

Important Roblox safety settings

1) Enable parental controls and age settings

2) Restrict or turn off chat with strangers where possible

3) Limit friend requests

4) Review privacy and communication settings regularly

5) Review friend list and recent contacts often

Red flags in Roblox

  • Players asking your child to move to Discord
  • Private servers with unknown players
  • Older players offering gifts or Robux
  • Requests to keep conversations secret
  • Repeated contact from the same player across games
  • Pressure to share personal details, photos, or age
