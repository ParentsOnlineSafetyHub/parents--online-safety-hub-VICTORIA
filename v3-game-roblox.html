Roblox

One of the most popular games for children — and one of the most commonly used for grooming contact.

Why Roblox matters

Roblox is not just a game. It is a massive social platform with millions of player-created worlds.

Children can interact with strangers through chat, private servers and friend systems.

Most grooming cases begin with casual conversations inside games

Important Roblox safety settings

1 Enable account restrictions

2 Turn off chat with strangers

3 Limit friend requests

4 Disable private servers where possible

5 Review friend list regularly

Red flags in Roblox

  • Players asking your child to move to Discord
  • Private servers with unknown players
  • Older players offering gifts or Robux
  • Requests to keep conversations secret
