Rec Room

Rec Room is a social VR game where players interact using voice chat.

Why Rec Room can expose children

Rec Room connects children to strangers through live voice chat and social spaces.

Players can enter rooms with unknown users and interact in real time.

Live voice chat increases risk

Important safety settings

Enable Junior Account where possible

Restrict voice chat

Disable friend requests from strangers

Avoid public rooms

Red flags

  • Adults befriending children
  • Invitations to private rooms
  • Players asking to move to Discord
  • Requests for personal information
