Rust

Rust is an open survival game with voice chat and large public servers.

Why parents should know Rust

Rust servers can contain hundreds of players communicating freely.

Voice chat and player interactions are completely open.

Open voice chat means direct contact with strangers

Common risks

  • Voice chat with strangers
  • Adult language and behaviour
  • Large online communities
  • Discord groups tied to servers

Parent safety steps

1) Disable voice chat where possible

2) Avoid large public servers

3) Check Steam friends regularly
