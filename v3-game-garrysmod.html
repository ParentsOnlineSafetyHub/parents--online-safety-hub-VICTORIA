Garry's Mod

A sandbox game with user-created servers and community mods.

Why parents should know Garry's Mod

Garry's Mod is a sandbox environment where players create their own games, worlds, and communities.

Server moderation varies widely.

User-created servers can expose children to adult content

Risks

  • Unmoderated servers
  • Voice chat
  • Player-created game modes
  • Private communities

Parent steps

1) Ask which servers are used

2) Monitor Steam friends

3) Avoid unknown community servers
