What makes a game higher risk?

  • Open voice chat
  • Private messages
  • Friend requests from strangers
  • Private servers or rooms
  • User-generated content or worlds
  • Trading, gifting, or item economy
  • Pressure to move to Discord, Snapchat, Telegram, or another app
  • Adults and children mixing in the same community spaces
  • Weak moderation or community-run servers

Important reminder for parents

A game does not need to look violent, sexual, or “bad” to be risky. The biggest risks usually come from communication, strangers, private contact, and off-platform movement.
