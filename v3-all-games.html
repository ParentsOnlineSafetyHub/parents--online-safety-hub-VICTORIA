What makes a game higher risk?

  • Open voice chat
  • Private messages
  • Friend requests from strangers
  • Private servers or rooms
  • User-generated content or worlds
  • Trading, gifting, or item economy
  • Pressure to move to Discord, Snapchat, or another app
