Is Bluesky Safe for Kids?

New platforms grow fast. Safety settings usually lag behind.

What is Bluesky?

A social media platform similar to Twitter/X where users post, follow, and interact publicly.

It is growing quickly, especially among younger users exploring alternatives.

Fast growth platforms often lack strong moderation early

Main risks

  • Public profiles by default
  • Direct messaging potential
  • Unknown followers
  • Exposure to adult content
  • Minimal parental awareness

What parents should do

  • Check if your child has an account
  • Set profile to private if possible
  • Discuss who they follow and accept
  • Watch for fast new “online friends”
New apps = higher risk until proven safe
