Extra Controls

  • Disable public microphone if possible.
  • Restrict access to public rooms.
  • Review friends lists regularly.
  • Avoid unsupervised public world hopping.
  • Monitor which communities or worlds children are joining.
VR spaces feel immersive. Children often forget they are speaking to strangers.

Important Warning About VRChat

VRChat is one of the most open VR platforms on the internet.

Public worlds allow anyone to join, including adults interacting with children through voice, avatars, and private rooms.

  • Unmoderated public voice chat is common.
  • Adult themed worlds and avatars exist.
  • Users may invite children into private instances.
  • External Discord servers are often used to continue conversations.
  • Avatars can perform explicit or inappropriate behaviour.
VRChat is generally not recommended for unsupervised children.

Parent Reminder

VR environments feel much more personal than normal games.

Children are not just typing messages. They are speaking, moving, and interacting in shared virtual spaces.

Immersion increases emotional influence and manipulation risk.
