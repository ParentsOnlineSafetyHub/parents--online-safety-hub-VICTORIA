Reporting in Victoria

If a child is in immediate danger: Call 000.

Before you report

  • Screenshot usernames, messages, and dates/times
  • Keep evidence organised in a folder
  • Block and report inside the app/platform
  • Do not confront the person directly (protect evidence)

Where to report

  • Victoria Police (for criminal matters and urgent concerns)
  • ACCCE (AFP) — Australian Centre to Counter Child Exploitation
  • eSafety Commissioner — for online abuse and platform complaints

Tip: Reporting is easier when evidence is organised. Keep screenshots + short notes of what happened.
