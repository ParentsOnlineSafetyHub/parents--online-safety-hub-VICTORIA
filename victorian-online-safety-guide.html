1. The 10-Minute Setup

  • Set all accounts to Private
  • Messaging: Friends Only
  • Disable voice chat for younger children
  • Turn off location sharing
  • Devices charge overnight in shared family area

2. Family Online Rules

  • No moving chats to new apps without parent approval
  • No sharing personal details (school, suburb, routines)
  • No sending photos to online-only contacts
  • Weekly calm check-in: “Anything weird happen online?”

3. Warning Signs of Grooming

  • Requests for secrecy
  • Gift offers (skins, credits, money)
  • Encouraging chat to move to another platform
  • Asking for photos or video
  • Flattery that becomes sexual

4. If You Are Concerned

  • Stay calm
  • Screenshot everything (usernames, dates, messages)
  • Block and report within the platform
  • If immediate danger: Call 000
  • Report to Victoria Police, ACCCE (AFP), or eSafety Commissioner

5. Ongoing Prevention

  • Review privacy settings monthly
  • Keep devices updated
  • Maintain open communication
  • Model healthy digital behaviour
