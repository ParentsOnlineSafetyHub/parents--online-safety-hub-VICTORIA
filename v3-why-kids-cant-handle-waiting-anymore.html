Why Kids Can’t Handle Waiting Anymore

Waiting used to be normal. Now it feels uncomfortable.

What parents notice

“How long?” constantly
Gets frustrated quickly
Needs instant answers
Struggles with delays

Why it’s happening

Instant videos
Instant replies
Instant entertainment
Instant reward loops

The pattern

Instant reward
Brain adapts
Delay feels harder
Frustration rises
Avoid waiting

Why it matters

Life requires waiting. Without it, frustration increases and resilience drops.
