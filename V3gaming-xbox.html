Xbox Platform Guide

Lock the platform first. Then lock the game.

Start Here

Xbox safety settings are controlled through the Microsoft Family Safety system. These settings manage communication, multiplayer access, spending, and friend requests.

Xbox Platform Checklist (V3)

Platform Setup (Quick Steps)

  • Open Settings → Account → Family Settings.
  • Enable Xbox Family Safety controls.
  • Restrict communication to friends only.
  • Set spending approvals and purchase limits.
  • Review friends list regularly.
If menus look different, use Settings search and type: family, privacy, or communication.

Associated Games (Lock These Next)
