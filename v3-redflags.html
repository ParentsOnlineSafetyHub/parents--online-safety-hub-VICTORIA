Predators & Grooming

Know the tactics. Predators follow patterns.

The Golden Rule

If someone asks your child to move a conversation off the platform to Discord, Snapchat, Telegram, WhatsApp or Instagram DMs that is a major grooming red flag.

Children should immediately tell a parent.

Moving chats off-platform is the most common grooming tactic.

How Grooming Usually Happens

Most predators do not start with inappropriate behaviour. They slowly build trust first.

  • 1 — Finding Access
    Predators search public games, servers, chats and comments where children interact.
  • 2 — Building Trust
    They act friendly and supportive. They mirror the child’s interests.
  • 3 — Isolation
    They move conversations into private messages.
  • 4 — Secrecy
    They encourage the child to keep the relationship secret.
  • 5 — Off-Platform Move
    They suggest Discord, Snapchat or Telegram.
  • 6 — Boundary Testing
    They test whether the child will follow instructions.
  • 7 — Sexualisation
    They introduce sexual content or conversations.
  • 8 — Exploitation
    They may use manipulation, pressure or blackmail.
Important: Grooming is rarely fast. It can take weeks or months.
