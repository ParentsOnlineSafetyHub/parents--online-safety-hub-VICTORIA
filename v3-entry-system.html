Quick concern check

Answer based on what is true right now.

1) Does something already feel wrong?
2) Has your child been hiding, deleting, or protecting messages or chats?
3) Is there a specific person, app, game, or “online friend” you are worried about?
4) Has your child been asked to keep something secret, move apps, or talk privately?
5) What do you need most right now?
