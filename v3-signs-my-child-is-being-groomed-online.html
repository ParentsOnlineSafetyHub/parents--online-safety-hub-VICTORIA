Signs My Child Is Being Groomed Online

If you’re searching this, something probably already feels wrong. Trust that instinct — then look for patterns.

The key truth

Grooming is not obvious at the start.

It builds slowly through trust, attention, and secrecy.

It often looks “normal” before it looks dangerous

Common signs

  • Increased secrecy
  • Emotional attachment to someone online
  • Defensive behaviour about one person
  • Moving conversations to private apps
  • Sudden mood changes
  • Deleting chats or hiding screens

What grooming often sounds like

“They understand me”

“Don’t tell anyone”

“Your parents won’t get it”

“This is just between us”
