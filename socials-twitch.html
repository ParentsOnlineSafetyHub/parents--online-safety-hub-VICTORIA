Primary Risk Areas

  • ☐ Live chat with adults
  • ☐ Private messages (whispers/DMs)
  • ☐ Exposure to mature content
  • ☐ Links and giveaways that lead off-platform

Immediate Lockdown Settings

  • ☐ Restrict DMs/whispers from strangers
  • ☐ Use follower-only chat where possible
  • ☐ Turn on moderation filters (spam/harassment)
  • ☐ Monitor who they follow and what they watch

House Rule

No private chats with streamers or strangers. No clicking giveaway links without approval.
