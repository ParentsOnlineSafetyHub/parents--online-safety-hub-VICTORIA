Virtual Reality

VR creates immersive voice environments. Children interact with strangers in real-time spaces.

Why VR exposure is high

VR environments allow players to speak naturally and interact as avatars.

Private rooms and voice chat can build trust very quickly.

VR voice chat environments can escalate contact faster than most games

VR safety settings to check

1) Restrict voice chat where possible

2) Disable private room invites from strangers

3) Keep younger users out of public worlds

4) Monitor friend lists and followers

5) Avoid unsupervised VR use for younger children

Common VR games parents should check

VRChat Rec Room Gorilla Tag

What parents should watch for

  • Adults spending unusual time with children
  • Private room invitations
  • Players encouraging outside contact
  • Requests to move to Discord or other apps
