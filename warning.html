Warning Signs of Online Grooming

Grooming often starts small and escalates. Teach kids to spot patterns.

  • Requests for secrecy: “Don’t tell your parents.”
  • Pushing chats to other apps (Discord/Snap/WhatsApp).
  • Gifts, credits, skins, or money offers.
  • Boundary testing that becomes sexual.
  • Requests for photos, video, or private calls.
  • Questions about school, suburb, routines, or where they’ll be.

What to do if you see these

  • Stay calm — thank your child for telling you.
  • Screenshot evidence (usernames, dates, messages).
  • Block and report within the platform.
  • Use official reporting pathways (see Reporting page).
