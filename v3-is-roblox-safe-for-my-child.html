Is Roblox Safe for My Child?

Roblox is not the problem. Unrestricted interaction inside it can be.

The reality

Millions of kids use Roblox safely.

But it also allows chat, strangers, and private interaction.

The risk comes from communication, not just gameplay

Main risks

  • Private messaging
  • Voice chat
  • Moving to Discord or other apps
  • “Free Robux” scams
  • Strangers building trust

How to make it safer

Turn off chat where possible

Use parental controls

Only allow known friends

Monitor gameplay

Talk regularly about interactions
