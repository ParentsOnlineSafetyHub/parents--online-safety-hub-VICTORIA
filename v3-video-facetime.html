Why FaceTime needs supervision

FaceTime is often “family safe”, but it still allows direct video contact. If an Apple ID/email is shared, calls can come through.

Step-by-step: reduce risk

1) Screen Time restrictions
Settings → Screen Time → Content & Privacy Restrictions → Allowed Apps → control FaceTime access.

2) Use contacts-only rule
Only accept FaceTime calls from real-life known contacts.

3) Keep calls in shared areas
No private bedroom calls for younger kids.

4) Apple ID safety
Keep Apple ID password private. Use Family Sharing. Avoid sharing login details widely.
