Start in 10 Minutes

These quick wins reduce risk immediately — even if you’re not tech-savvy.

  • Set accounts to Private
  • Messaging: Friends only
  • Turn off or restrict friend requests
  • Disable voice chat for younger children
  • Turn off location sharing
  • Devices charge overnight in a shared family area

Tip: Do a calm weekly check-in: “Anything weird happen online this week?”
