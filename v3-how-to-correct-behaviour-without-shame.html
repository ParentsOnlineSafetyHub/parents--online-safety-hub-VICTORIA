How to Correct Behaviour Without Shame

Shame stops communication.
Correction should build awareness, not silence.

The problem with shame

Shame creates shutdown

Shutdown reduces honesty

Reduced honesty increases risk

What to avoid

  • “What were you thinking?”
  • “You should know better”
  • Public embarrassment

What to say instead

“Let’s look at what happened”

“What was going on in that moment?”

“What can we do differently next time?”

Why this works

  • Keeps communication open
  • Builds decision-making skills
  • Reduces repeated behaviour
