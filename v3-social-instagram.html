Instagram

Reels + DMs = risk. Lock privacy, messages, and discoverability.

Highest risks

1) DMs from unknown accounts

2) Reels (short-form recommendation loop)

3) Public profile discovery

4) Comment threads

Quick safety locks

1) Private account ON

2) Message requests restricted

3) Limit who can comment/tag/mention

4) Turn off location sharing

5) Review follower list weekly

Why algorithms matter

Reels works like Shorts/TikTok. Limiting the loop reduces exposure creep.
