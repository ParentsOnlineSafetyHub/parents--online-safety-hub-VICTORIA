Xbox Safety Guide

Lock the platform first. Then lock each game.

What matters on Xbox

Xbox controls can restrict: chat, multiplayer, spending, friends, content ratings, and time limits. Platform controls are the strongest layer.

Step-by-step (quick setup)

1) Add child to Microsoft Family

Create/confirm your Microsoft Family group and add your child account.

2) Set age-appropriate content

Family settings → Child → Content restrictions → set age rating.

3) Lock communication

Set: • Who can communicate with voice/text → Friends only (or Block)
• Who can add friends → restrict as needed

4) Spending controls

Require approval for purchases. Disable/limit store access.

5) Time limits

Set daily limits + bedtime lockouts. Consistency beats arguments.
