Why support POSH

POSH is built to take the confusion out for overwhelmed parents and point them toward the right action faster.

Support helps keep guides updated, printable resources available, new pages built, and better safety tools added over time.

Support helps get better child safety information in front of more parents
Why this matters:
The easier child safety is to understand, the more likely parents are to act early instead of waiting until something has already gone wrong.

What support helps fund

  • New safety pages for games, apps, and platforms
  • Printable PDF downloads for parents and carers
  • Site upkeep, hosting, design, and improvements
  • Awareness resources that help parents recognise warning signs earlier
  • Future tools that make child safety guidance easier to use
POSH is not backed by a large organisation. Support helps keep the mission moving.
