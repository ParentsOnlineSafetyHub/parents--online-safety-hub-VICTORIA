What support for POSH really means

POSH is not just a website. It is a growing child safety platform built to help parents understand risk earlier, reduce confusion, and move toward the right next step faster.

Support helps keep that mission moving. It helps build more pages, more tools, more downloads, more practical guidance, and better ways for families to find help when they feel overwhelmed.

Support for POSH is support for earlier awareness, clearer guidance, and stronger family protection.

Why support POSH

POSH is built to take confusion out for overwhelmed parents and point them toward the right action faster.

Support helps keep guides updated, printable resources available, new pages built, and better safety tools added over time.

Support helps get better child safety information in front of more parents

Why this matters:
The easier child safety is to understand, the more likely parents are to act early instead of waiting until something has already gone wrong.

What support helps build

  • New safety pages for games, apps, devices, platforms, and behaviour risks
  • Printable PDF downloads for parents, carers, and families
  • Site upkeep, hosting, design, structure, and ongoing improvements
  • Awareness tools that help parents recognise warning signs earlier
  • Future systems that make child safety guidance faster and easier to use
POSH is not backed by a large organisation. Support helps keep the mission moving.
