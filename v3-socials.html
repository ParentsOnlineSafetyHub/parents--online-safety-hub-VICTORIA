Social Media & Chat

If it has DMs, groups, or disappearing messages — it needs locking and supervision.

Golden Rule

If someone asks your child to move to another app, private server, or disappearing-message platform, that is a red flag.

Private contact reduces visibility and increases grooming risk.

DMs + secrecy + disappearing messages = higher risk

High exposure apps

Discord Snapchat Instagram TikTok YouTube

Other apps to lock down

Facebook Messenger Messenger Kids Telegram WhatsApp Twitch Steam YouTube Kids Reddit Pinterest

Quick rules for every social app

1) Private account by default.

2) DMs = friends-only.

3) Location sharing OFF.

4) No disappearing messages for kids.

5) Parents check settings weekly (5 minutes).

Parent line: “I’m not checking because I don’t trust you. I’m checking because strangers don’t get private access to you.”
