Primary Risk Areas

  • ☐ Anonymous usernames
  • ☐ Private groups/channels
  • ☐ Encrypted chats (harder to supervise)
  • ☐ Unknown users adding them to groups

Immediate Lockdown Settings

  • ☐ Phone number visibility → Nobody
  • ☐ Who can add to groups → My Contacts
  • ☐ Calls → My Contacts
  • ☐ Disable unknown message requests
  • ☐ Review joined groups/channels monthly

House Rule

No joining channels/groups without parent approval. No private chats with strangers.
