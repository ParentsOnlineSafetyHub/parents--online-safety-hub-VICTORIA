Why Messenger Requires Attention

Messenger allows private chats, voice calls, video calls, disappearing messages, and group chats. Strangers can attempt contact if privacy settings are weak.

Step-By-Step Safety Settings

1) Set Message Controls
Open Messenger → Tap Profile Picture → Privacy & Safety → Message Delivery.
Set "Others on Facebook" to Message Requests or Don't Receive Requests.

2) Restrict Who Can Add Your Child to Groups
Privacy & Safety → Message Delivery → Set “Everyone” to Message Requests.

3) Turn Off Active Status
Profile Picture → Active Status → Turn Off.
This prevents strangers knowing when your child is online.

4) Disable Story Replies from Strangers
Settings → Privacy → Story → Set replies to Friends Only.

5) Review Friends List
Remove anyone not known in real life.

House Rule Recommendation

No private chats with anyone not known offline. No deleting chat history without a parent knowing.
