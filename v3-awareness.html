Predator Tactics Breakdown

This is how grooming typically escalates across Roblox, Discord, VRChat, Snapchat, Telegram and other chat/video apps. The moment a stranger pushes secrecy or tries to move your child off-platform — treat it as a red flag.

Golden Rule: Don’t move chats off-platform
Secrecy = Danger
Kids won’t be in trouble for telling you
1

Target & Find Access

They search where kids are: open games, public servers, voice chat, group chats, comments, “kid friendly” spaces.

2

Trust Building

Friendly mentor vibe. Compliments. “You’re mature for your age.” Finds what your child likes and mirrors it.

3

Isolation

Pulls them away from public spaces into DMs, private servers, private worlds, or voice chat.

4

Secrecy & Control

“Don’t tell your parents.” “They won’t understand.” “You’ll get banned.” This is where kids stop speaking up.

5

Off-Platform Luring

“Add me on Discord/Snap/Telegram/WhatsApp.” This reduces moderation and evidence trails. Major escalation point.

6

Boundary Testing

Small “jokes,” dares, or “questions.” They check if your child will comply and stay quiet.

7

Sexualisation & Desensitisation

Early porn exposure, sexual talk, “education,” grooming language, pushing shame and secrecy.

8

Coercion & Blackmail

Screenshots, threats, humiliation, “I’ll tell your parents,” “I’ll post it,” “You started it.” This is exploitation.

What parents do (simple & effective)

Rule: No secret accounts. Parents can check anytime.
Rule: Friends list = real-life people only.
Rule: If someone asks to move apps → tell parent immediately.
Setup: Lock DMs, voice, invites to friends-only (or OFF).
Safety: Devices charge overnight in a shared area.
Trust: “You will not be in trouble for telling me.”

Short: Why Roblox, Discord & VRChat are high exposure

Roblox: massive kid audience, open communities, DMs/party chat, user-generated content, and fast friend adding.

Discord: private servers, DMs, voice calls, disappearing or hidden communities, and easy “move off-platform” lures.

VRChat: voice-first + immersive contact, adults and kids mixing, private worlds, and fast escalation into isolation.
