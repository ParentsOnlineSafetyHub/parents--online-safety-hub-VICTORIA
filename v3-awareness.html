High Exposure: Roblox • Discord • VRChat

High social contact + DMs + voice + communities = risk

Why these rank high: strangers can access kids through chat, groups, voice, friend requests, and “hangout” style spaces.

Common pattern: friendly contact → private chat → secrecy → sexual content / coercion → off-platform move.

Kids should never be punished for telling the truth.

What grooming looks like (simple signs)

  • Flattery + attention (“you’re mature”, “you’re special”, “no one understands you like I do”).
  • Secrecy (“don’t tell your parents”, “they’ll take your device”).
  • Gifts / in-game currency / skins / “I’ll buy you stuff”.
  • Private calls, DMs, “late-night chats”, or constant messaging.
  • Sexual topics: porn, masturbation, body changes, “truth or dare”, “send pics”.
  • Pressure to move off-platform (Discord/Snapchat/Telegram/WhatsApp).
  • Threats, guilt, blackmail, or “you’ll get in trouble if you tell”.
Parent baseline: If it has chat/DMs/calls/groups — it must be locked down and checked regularly.

If your child tells you something happened

  • Stay calm. Your reaction decides whether they ever tell you again.
  • Reassure: “You’re not in trouble. I’m proud you told me.”
  • Save evidence: screenshots, usernames, server/group names, dates/times.
  • Block + report inside the app/platform immediately.
  • Escalate: go to Reporting and follow the steps.
