The Golden Rule

No private chats with strangers.
Predators try to move kids to private apps, encrypted chats, disappearing messages, or voice/video.

Why platforms like Roblox, Discord, VRChat are high exposure

  • Open communities: strangers can contact kids via chat, voice, groups, servers, rooms.
  • Identity masking: adults can pretend to be kids; usernames hide real identity.
  • Migration risk: “Add me on Snapchat/Telegram/Discord” = the classic move.
  • Algorithm + recommendations: kids get served content and communities they didn’t search for.

What to teach kids (simple, repeatable)

  • Never share name, school, suburb, photos, location, or schedules.
  • No “secret friends.” No disappearing messages.
  • If they feel weird, pressured, flattered, or scared — tell a parent immediately.
  • Screenshot evidence. Block. Report. Tell an adult.
