POSH

Mindset

Calm parents create safe kids. Consistency beats intensity.

The 3-second pause

Before you react — pause for 3 seconds. Calm responses make kids more likely to tell you the truth when it matters.

Non-negotiable parenting principles

1) Protect first. Lecture later.

2) No shame. No ridicule. No “I told you so”.

3) Boundaries are love — when they’re consistent.

4) If a child tells you something scary, your calm is their safety.

Better questions (build trust)

• “What’s something you like about yourself?”

• “When you mess up, what do you say to yourself?”

• “What do you do when you feel sad?”

• “Is there anything you’re scared to tell me?”

• “If you could change one thing right now, what would it be?”

Practical next step

When you’re calm, go lock the settings. Prevention is the win.
