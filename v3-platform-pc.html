PC Gaming

PC has the widest exposure of any platform.

Why PC needs strong controls

PC allows access to games, chat apps, downloads, browsers and private messaging.

That makes it one of the most important platforms for parents to secure properly.

Open PC access = open internet exposure

Important PC safety checks

1) Create a dedicated child account

2) Restrict software downloads

3) Limit explicit web content

4) Review camera and microphone permissions

5) Check installed chat apps regularly

Many risks on PC come from **chat platforms, mods, and downloaded apps**, not just the games themselves.

Common PC games and apps parents should check

Roblox Minecraft Fortnite Discord VRChat Steam

What parents should watch for

  • Unknown contacts appearing in chat apps
  • Children joining Discord servers or private groups
  • Requests to move conversations off platforms
  • Downloads of unknown apps, mods or files
  • Voice chat with strangers during gameplay
