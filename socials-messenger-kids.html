What Parents Need To Know

  • ☐ Messenger Kids is parent-controlled, but you must keep contacts tight.
  • ☐ The main risk is who gets approved as a contact, and group chat exposure.

Step-by-Step (Parent Setup)

  • ☐ Only approve contacts your child knows in real life (family/real friends).
  • ☐ Keep contact approval turned on (parent-only control).
  • ☐ Restrict/avoid group chats unless necessary.
  • ☐ Review contacts monthly and remove anyone no longer appropriate.

House Rule

No calls/chats unless the contact is parent-approved. If anything feels weird or secretive, tell a parent immediately.
