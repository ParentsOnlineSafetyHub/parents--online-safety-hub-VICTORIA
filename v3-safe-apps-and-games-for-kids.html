Safe Apps and Games for Kids

No app is 100% safe. Safety comes from settings, supervision, and awareness.

Important truth

Even “kid-friendly” apps can include chat, strangers, or hidden risks.

Safety depends on how the app is used — not just its name.

Safe platform does not mean safe behaviour

Safer types of apps

  • Educational apps (no chat features)
  • Offline games
  • Creative apps (drawing, music, building)
  • Games with restricted communication

Higher-risk features to watch

  • Private messaging
  • Voice chat
  • Friend requests from strangers
  • Cross-platform communication
  • In-game trading or gifting
Risk comes from interaction, not just the app itself.

What parents should do

Check settings before allowing use

Disable chat where possible

Know who your child plays with

Set clear rules about adding friends

Keep conversations open
