PlayStation Safety Guide

Platform controls first. Then game settings.

What matters on PlayStation

PlayStation controls can restrict: chat, friend requests, spending, content ratings, and time limits.

Step-by-step (quick setup)

1) Create a child account under Family Management

Settings → Family and Parental Controls → Family Management → Add child.

2) Set age rating restrictions

Parental controls → Age level for games → choose child-appropriate level.

3) Restrict communication

Disable/limit: • Chat/messages
• Voice communication
• Friend requests

4) Spending controls

Set purchase limits and require password at checkout.

5) Time limits

Use play time restrictions so gaming stays healthy.
