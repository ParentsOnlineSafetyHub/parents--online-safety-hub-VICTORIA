PlayStation

Lock the console first. Then check the games your child actually plays.

Why PlayStation settings matter

PlayStation controls messages, friend requests, party chat, purchases, and who can contact your child.

Those settings should be locked before relying on the game’s own settings.

Console settings are one of the strongest first safety layers

Important PlayStation settings

1) Restrict messages from strangers

2) Limit friend requests

3) Lock purchases and wallet spending

4) Restrict voice chat exposure

5) Use family or child account controls where possible

Common PlayStation games parents should check

Fortnite Roblox Minecraft Call of Duty GTA Online Rocket League Apex Legends Overwatch Rainbow Six Siege

What parents should watch for

  • Unknown friend requests after online matches
  • Children joining party chat with strangers
  • Pressure to move to Discord or another app
  • Late-night online sessions with regular unknown players

Next safety steps

Don’t stop at the console. Check the games, the chat apps, and the warning signs too.
