PlayStation

Lock the console first. Then check the games your child actually plays.

What to lock first on PlayStation

PlayStation settings can control communication, friend requests, purchases, and who can contact your child.

These settings matter before you even open the game itself.

Messages, voice chat, and friend requests matter most

PlayStation settings to check now

1) Restrict messages and private communication

2) Limit who can send friend requests

3) Lock purchases and wallet spending

4) Review voice chat exposure in games

5) Use a child/family account where possible

Common PlayStation games to check

Fortnite Roblox Minecraft Call of Duty GTA / Mature Games Rocket League
