Why Skype needs supervision

Skype allows direct voice/video calls and chat. The main risks are unknown contacts, link-based calls, and kids accepting requests.

Step-by-step: reduce risk

1) Lock down who can contact your child
Skype → Settings → Privacy (or Contacts) → restrict contact requests where possible.
Rule: Only real-life known contacts.

2) Turn off “discoverability”
If Skype offers options like being searchable by phone/email, turn that off or limit it.

3) Block unknown contacts immediately
Open chat → tap profile → Block + Report (if available).

4) Supervise video calls for younger kids
Keep calls in shared spaces. No bedroom calls.

5) Device-level controls first
Use Apple Screen Time / Google Family Link to restrict app installs and time windows.

House rule

No video calls with strangers. Ever. If someone asks to “keep it secret”, that’s a red flag.
