Why this guide matters

Online child safety is not just a local issue. It affects families everywhere.

This guide exists to help parents recognise risks earlier, respond calmly, and protect children across games, apps, chats, livestreams, and online communities.

The risks may look different, but the patterns stay the same

What this guide helps parents do

  • Understand how online grooming and manipulation often begins
  • Recognise warning signs earlier
  • Set stronger device, platform, and game boundaries
  • Respond calmly if something already feels wrong
  • Share practical safety knowledge with other families
The goal is not fear. The goal is awareness, clarity, and child protection.
