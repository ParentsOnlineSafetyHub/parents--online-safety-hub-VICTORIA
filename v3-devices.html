Devices

Start with the device in your child’s hand. Lock the device first, then the apps, games, and platforms on it.

Why devices come first

If the device is open, everything on it is easier to access.

Parental controls, install restrictions, content filters, and permissions should be the first safety layer.

Device first = stronger protection everywhere else

Choose your child’s device

Different devices have different settings, risks, and control systems.

iPhone Android Phone iPad / Tablet Windows PC

What to lock on every device

1) App installs and purchases

2) Screen time / downtime

3) Web content and explicit content filters

4) Camera, microphone, and location permissions

5) Parent-controlled passcode / settings access
