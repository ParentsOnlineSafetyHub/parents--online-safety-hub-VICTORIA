Is WeChat Safe for Kids?

Messaging + social + payments. Multiple features mean multiple risks.

What is WeChat?

A messaging app that also includes social feeds, groups, and additional features.

Used widely in different communities worldwide.

Multiple features increase exposure risk

Main risks

  • Group chats with unknown people
  • Private messaging
  • Social feed exposure
  • Contact sharing

What parents should check

  • Who your child is messaging
  • Group memberships
  • Privacy settings
  • Contacts added
