Is Discord Safe for Kids?

Discord is not designed specifically for children. It allows private servers, chats, and strangers.

Main risks

  • Private messaging
  • Strangers joining servers
  • Voice chat
  • Moving to other apps
Most risks happen through communication, not the platform itself

How to make it safer

Disable direct messages

Only allow known servers

Monitor usage

Talk about who they interact with
