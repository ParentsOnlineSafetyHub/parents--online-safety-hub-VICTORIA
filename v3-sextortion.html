Sextortion Awareness

Sextortion is one of the fastest growing online crimes targeting teenagers.

What Is Sextortion?

Sextortion happens when someone tricks a child or teenager into sending private images and then threatens to share them unless the victim sends more images or money.

This is a crime and a form of exploitation.

How Sextortion Usually Starts

  • Fake profile contacts the teen
  • Often pretending to be another teenager
  • Conversation becomes flirtatious
  • Requests private images
  • Threats begin after the image is sent

What Predators Threaten

  • Sending images to friends
  • Posting images online
  • Sending images to parents
  • Demanding money or more images

What Parents Should Do

  • Stay calm
  • Do NOT blame the child
  • Save evidence
  • Report the account
  • Contact authorities if necessary
Children in sextortion situations feel extreme fear. Support and reassurance are critical.
