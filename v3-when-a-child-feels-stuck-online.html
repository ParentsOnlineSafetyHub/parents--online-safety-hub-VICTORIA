When a Child Feels Stuck Online

Feeling stuck is a warning sign.
It means the child no longer feels in control of the situation.

What “stuck” actually means

They don’t want to continue

But feel like they can’t stop

They don’t want to tell

But feel like they need to hide it

Being stuck often leads to silence and escalation.

Why kids feel stuck

  • Emotional attachment
  • Fear of consequences
  • Pressure or manipulation
  • Guilt or responsibility
  • Threats or blackmail

What it can look like

  • Constant checking of messages
  • Stress when they can’t respond
  • Mood changes after being online
  • Protecting one person or chat
  • Fear when asked about it

What parents should do

Stay calm

Remove pressure, not control

Help them feel safe to tell

Interrupt the situation early

The goal is to give the child a safe exit, not trap them further.
