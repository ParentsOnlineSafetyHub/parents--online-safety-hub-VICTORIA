Algorithm Awareness

How algorithms on games and social apps can affect your child’s exposure to risk.

Why algorithms matter

Platforms like TikTok, Roblox, and YouTube use algorithmic feeds to keep kids engaged. These algorithms may unintentionally amplify unsafe content or push interactions that predators can exploit.

Understanding algorithmic influence helps parents set boundaries and know what to watch.
