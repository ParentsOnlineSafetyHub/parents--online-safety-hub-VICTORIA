Device Safety

Your strongest first layer of protection. Lock the device first, then the platform, then the app.

Why device safety comes first

If the device is open, every app, game, browser, and message system is easier to access.

Strong device settings reduce exposure before a child even opens a game or social app.

Device first = stronger safety everywhere else

What to lock on every device

1) Restrict app installs and purchases

2) Set screen time, downtime, or family controls

3) Filter explicit content and unsafe browsing

4) Review location, camera, and microphone permissions

5) Use a parent-controlled passcode or settings lock

Choose your child’s device

iPhone Android Phone iPad / Tablet Windows PC

What parents often miss

  • Children can use browsers instead of apps
  • Location settings stay on without parents realising
  • Messaging apps may be hidden in folders or secondary accounts
  • Late-night device use often increases secrecy and risk
