POSH

VRChat

VRChat mixes children and adults. Voice chat and private worlds increase risk.

High Exposure Warning

VRChat allows voice communication between strangers in shared virtual environments.

Private worlds and voice chat can allow predators to isolate children.

Private worlds + voice chat = higher risk

Safety Setup

  • Disable unknown friend requests
  • Restrict voice chat to known friends
  • Disable invites from strangers
  • Monitor friends list regularly
  • Limit play time and device access

Parent Rules

  • Only play with real-life friends
  • No private worlds with strangers
  • No sharing real name, school or location
  • If someone asks to move chats to Discord → tell a parent
