Why VRChat is high exposure

VRChat is built around voice chat, public rooms, private rooms, and adult users mixing into the same spaces. Kids can be approached quickly, then pulled into private worlds or off-platform messaging.

Grooming: warning signs Predators & off-platform luring

Non-negotiables (fastest protection)

1) No private worlds with strangers.

2) Voice/mic rules: mute or friends-only wherever possible.

3) If anyone asks for secrecy, photos, or to move apps → leave and tell a parent.

4) Use device-level controls and keep VR in shared spaces at home.

Platforms hub (VR settings) Device Controls

Home setup rules for VR

Shared space only (not behind closed doors).

Time limits (short sessions, breaks, no late-night VR).

Check-ins: parent asks what worlds they visited and who they spoke to (calmly).
