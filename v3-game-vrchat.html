VRChat

VRChat is one of the highest exposure environments for children online.

Why VRChat is high exposure

VRChat is a live social world with voice chat and full interaction.

Children can easily interact with adults in private rooms.

Voice + immersion = fast trust building

Safety precautions

Avoid public worlds

Disable voice chat where possible

Do not allow private room invites

Supervise use

Major red flags

  • Adults befriending children in VR
  • Private world invites
  • Requests to move to Discord
  • Requests for photos or personal info
