Primary Risk Areas

  • ☐ Direct messages from unknown users
  • ☐ Public boards revealing personal interests
  • ☐ Links leading to external sites
  • ☐ Algorithm exposing mature or harmful content
  • ☐ Private sharing of boards

Immediate Lockdown Settings

  • ☐ Set account to Private (if appropriate)
  • ☐ Restrict who can message
  • ☐ Review followers regularly
  • ☐ Disable personalised ads (where possible)
  • ☐ Monitor boards for oversharing personal information

House Rule

No sharing personal details (school, location, routines). No clicking unknown links from messages.
