Report harmful/illegal content online

eSafety can help remove the most seriously harmful content online, including child sexual abuse material. Use their reporting portal and follow the form that matches the issue. 3

Australian Centre to Counter Child Exploitation (ACCCE)

To report online child sexual abuse material/activity, ACCCE provides a Report Abuse pathway. 4

ACCCE: Report Abuse

Evidence checklist (do this calmly)

1) Screenshot messages/usernames (include dates/times).

2) Record profile links + platform name + server/group name.

3) Don’t warn the offender. Block/report and preserve evidence.

4) If money/extortion involved: stop contact and report immediately.
