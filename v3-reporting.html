Immediate danger

  • ☐ Call 000 if a child is in immediate danger.
  • ☐ Preserve evidence: screenshots, usernames, timestamps.

Online safety steps

  • ☐ Block the account immediately.
  • ☐ Report inside the platform/app.
  • ☐ Save links, profiles, chat logs.
  • ☐ Inform other parent/guardian if relevant.
