Nintendo Switch

Nintendo feels safer to many parents, but it still needs proper settings and supervision.

Do not assume “Nintendo” means low risk

Even child-friendly platforms can still expose children to voice chat, strangers, messaging, online play, and YouTube-style content.

It still needs rules, parental controls, and app/game checks.

Child-friendly does not mean risk-free

Nintendo settings to check now

1) Set up parental controls in the Nintendo app

2) Restrict purchases and eShop access

3) Review online play permissions

4) Watch for games that connect children to strangers

5) Check extra apps like YouTube if installed

Common Nintendo games / apps to check

Fortnite Minecraft YouTube Roblox / Browser Related
