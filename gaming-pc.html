PC — Core Safety Checklist

Main risks: Steam chat, Discord overlap, unmoderated servers, web access.

Immediate Protection

  • ☐ Windows/Mac parental controls enabled (age appropriate)
  • ☐ Game chat restricted (friends only where possible)
  • ☐ Steam privacy tightened
  • ☐ Browser safety settings enabled
  • ☐ Downloads/install permissions restricted for younger kids

House Rule

  • ☐ No adding strangers from servers
  • ☐ No moving chats to Telegram/Discord without parent approval
