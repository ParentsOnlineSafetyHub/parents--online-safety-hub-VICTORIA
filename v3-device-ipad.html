iPad / Tablet

Tablets feel harmless to many parents, but they still need proper controls, app restrictions, and screen rules.

Do not assume tablets are low risk

Tablets often become the “easy device” children use for games, YouTube, messaging, and browsing.

That makes them one of the most overlooked safety risks in the home.

A child-friendly device can still create child exposure

Lock these tablet settings first

1) Turn on parental controls / Screen Time

2) Restrict app installs and in-app purchases

3) Filter web content and video access

4) Review app permissions

5) Set clear rules for where and when the tablet is used

Common apps parents should check

YouTube YouTube Kids Roblox Minecraft Live Video & Streaming
