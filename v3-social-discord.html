Discord

Discord is the most common platform predators move children to from games.

Why Discord matters

Many predators move conversations from games to Discord because it allows private messaging and servers.

Once a child moves to Discord, monitoring becomes harder.

Moving chats off-platform is a major grooming signal

Important safety settings

Disable direct messages from server members

Limit friend requests

Avoid joining unknown servers

Review friend list regularly

Major red flags

  • Adults messaging children
  • Private voice chats
  • Requests for photos
  • Requests for secrecy
