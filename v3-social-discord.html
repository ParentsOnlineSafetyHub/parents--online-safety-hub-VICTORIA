Discord Safety

Private servers + DMs + voice chat make Discord one of the highest exposure platforms.

Golden Rule

If someone from a game asks your child to join Discord — treat it as a red flag.

Predators commonly move kids from Roblox, Fortnite, or Minecraft servers to Discord because moderation is weaker and conversations become private.

Game → Discord move is the most common grooming escalation.
How Grooming Works Red Flags Reporting

Quick Lockdown (do this first)

1. Open Discord → User Settings

2. Go to Privacy & Safety

3. Turn on Keep Me Safe

4. Turn OFF Allow DMs from server members

5. Turn OFF Friend requests from everyone

6. Go to Authorized Apps → remove unknown bots/apps

7. Disable Who can add you as a friend except real friends

High Risk Features

Private Servers — predators create servers designed for teens or gaming communities.

Direct Messages — strangers can privately message kids after meeting in games.

Voice Channels — private voice chats allow manipulation and grooming.

File Sharing — images, porn, and explicit content can be sent instantly.

Bot Invitations — bots can add unknown communities and links.

Signs something is wrong

  • Child suddenly joins private servers
  • Talking to “older friends” they met in games
  • Late-night voice calls
  • Switching accounts or hiding screens
  • Suddenly secretive about Discord
Important: Kids often hide this because they fear losing their device. Always make it safe for them to tell you.

Parent Rules That Work

Friends list = real-life friends only

No private servers without parent approval

No voice calls with strangers

If someone asks to move platforms → tell parent immediately

If your child is targeted

  • Do not delete messages
  • Take screenshots of usernames and server names
  • Report user inside Discord
  • Block immediately
  • Escalate to platform reporting if necessary

Why Discord is used by predators

Discord allows strangers to communicate privately with children through servers, DMs, and voice channels. Once a predator convinces a child to move to Discord, conversations become harder for parents and game moderators to see.

Many grooming cases begin in a game like Roblox and then move to Discord.
