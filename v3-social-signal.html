Is Signal Safe for Kids?

Privacy tools protect users — but also hide risk.

What is Signal?

An encrypted messaging app focused on privacy.

Messages are not easily visible to parents or monitoring tools.

Encrypted apps can hide conversations completely

Main risks

  • Hidden conversations
  • Disappearing messages
  • No visibility for parents
  • Direct contact with unknown people

What parents should do

  • Check if Signal is installed
  • Discuss why private apps can be risky
  • Set rules around messaging apps
  • Watch for secrecy or deleted chats
