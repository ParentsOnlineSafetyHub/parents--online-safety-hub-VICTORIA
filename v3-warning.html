Non-negotiables

  • ☐ No private chats with strangers.
  • ☐ No sharing photos, school info, address, or routines.
  • ☐ Devices charge overnight in a shared family space.
  • ☐ Parents keep admin passwords/PINs private.
  • ☐ If secrecy is demanded, it’s a red flag.

When to act immediately

  • ☐ Requests to move chat off-platform.
  • ☐ Gifts, codes, money offers.
  • ☐ Sexual language, threats, or manipulation.
  • ☐ Attempts to isolate the child from parents.
