Age Guide

Age appropriate access reduces risk dramatically.

Under 10

No public chat. No DMs. No open servers. Supervised gaming only. Device-level controls active.

10–13

Restricted accounts. Friends approved by parents. No private voice chat. Screen time boundaries enforced.

13–15

Gradual responsibility. Teach red flags. Review privacy settings together.

16+

Guided independence. Open communication > control.
