TikTok

Short-form video = fast exposure. Lock privacy, comments, and DMs.

Highest risks

1) Endless scroll + rapid recommendations

2) Comments + DMs

3) Lives and live chat

4) Public profiles and location signals

Quick safety locks

1) Private account ON

2) Comments restricted (friends-only) or OFF

3) DMs OFF or friends-only

4) Limit who can stitch/duet

5) Screen time limits + breaks

Why algorithms matter

Short-form recommendation systems can cause exposure creep fast. Parents reduce risk by limiting the loop.
