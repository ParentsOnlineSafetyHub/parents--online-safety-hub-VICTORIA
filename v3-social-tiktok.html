YouTube

Great learning tool — high exposure by default. Lock history, autoplay, and Shorts.

High risk areas

1) Shorts (endless scroll + rapid recommendations)

2) Comment sections (public usernames + replies)

3) Livestreams (live chat exposure)

4) Suggested videos (exposure creep over time)

Quick safety locks (do these first)

1) Turn OFF autoplay (stops the rabbit hole)

2) Keep the child on a child/supervised account (avoid adult accounts)

3) Limit or avoid Shorts for younger kids

4) Avoid comment sections and livestream chat

5) Weekly check: watch history + subscriptions

Why algorithms matter

Recommendation systems respond to behaviour patterns. Over time a child can be gradually recommended older content, older audiences, and more risky comment environments.
