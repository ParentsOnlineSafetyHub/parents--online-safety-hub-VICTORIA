TikTok

TikTok is driven by algorithm exposure. Content risks and private contact can escalate fast.

Why TikTok matters

TikTok can expose children to strangers, harmful trends, sexualised content, and live interactions very quickly.

Even passive scrolling teaches the algorithm what to show next.

A few clicks can change a child’s feed fast

Important TikTok settings

1) Set the account to Private

2) Restrict direct messages

3) Disable or limit duets, stitches, and downloads

4) Use family pairing where possible

5) Review who follows and comments

Major red flags on TikTok

  • Older users messaging children
  • Pressure to move to Instagram, Snapchat, or Telegram
  • Live videos with unsafe comments or private invites
  • Sexualised trends or risky challenge content
  • Rapid change in the type of content appearing in the feed
