If something feels wrong

Trust your instinct

Slow the situation down

Check the child calmly

Look for patterns, not excuses

Parents often sense something before they understand it

Immediate actions you can take

  • Ask your child calmly what is going on
  • Look at the device together (not secretly first)
  • Pause contact with the risky person
  • Save anything important before it disappears
  • Reduce access if needed (without punishment tone)
The goal is to slow things down, not explode the situation

What not to do

  • Do not panic or yell immediately
  • Do not accuse without understanding
  • Do not take the device and shut everything down instantly
  • Do not shame the child
  • Do not ignore your instinct
If the child feels unsafe telling you, they will hide the next problem
