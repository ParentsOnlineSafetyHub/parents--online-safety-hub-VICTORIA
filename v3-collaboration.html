About POSH

POSH stands for Parents Online Safety Hub.

It is an independent awareness platform designed to help parents better understand online risks involving games, apps, algorithms, private messaging, grooming pathways, and harmful content exposure.

The goal is simple:

Give parents clearer information, earlier awareness, and practical tools that help protect children before problems escalate.

What POSH focuses on

  • Online grooming pathways
  • Gaming platform communication risks
  • Social media and chat-app exposure
  • Movement from games into private messaging apps
  • Algorithm-driven exposure and escalation
  • Warning signs parents may miss
  • Practical parent guidance and scripts
