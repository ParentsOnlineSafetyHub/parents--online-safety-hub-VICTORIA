Best Parental Control Apps

No app replaces parenting. But the right tools can give visibility and early warning.

Popular options parents use

  • Google Family Link (Android)
  • Apple Screen Time (iPhone / iPad)
  • Qustodio
  • Bark
  • Norton Family
  • Microsoft Family Safety
Start with built-in tools before paying for advanced apps.

What these apps can do

  • Monitor screen time
  • Block apps or websites
  • Track downloads
  • Set usage limits
  • Alert risky behaviour

What they cannot do

  • See everything perfectly
  • Replace conversations
  • Stop all risk
  • Detect manipulation early on their own
Tools support awareness — they don’t replace it.
