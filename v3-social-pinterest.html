Pinterest

Visual discovery platform. Direct messages and mature content possible.

Why Pinterest still needs settings locked

Pinterest allows messaging, public profiles, and exposure to adult content depending on searches and algorithm suggestions.

Lock Pinterest properly

1) Set profile to private.

2) Disable messaging if not needed.

3) Turn off “Search privacy” risks (hide profile from search engines).

4) Monitor saved boards.

5) Avoid sharing personal photos publicly.

Recommended boundary

Pinterest is safer than many social apps but still requires supervision under 13.
