My Child Is Hiding Messages — What Should I Do?

Hiding messages is not always rebellion. Sometimes it is fear, pressure, or something they don’t know how to explain.

What it can mean

  • They don’t want to get in trouble
  • They feel embarrassed
  • Someone told them to keep it secret
  • They are unsure how to tell you
Secrecy increases risk, not just disobedience

What not to do

  • Don’t explode immediately
  • Don’t accuse without understanding
  • Don’t turn it into punishment first

What to do instead

Stay calm

Ask open questions

Check the device properly

Focus on safety
