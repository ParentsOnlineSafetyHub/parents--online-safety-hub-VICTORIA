What Parents Need To Know

  • ☐ Messenger allows private messages and calls.
  • ☐ Message requests from unknown people are the main risk.
  • ☐ Groups can add pressure and introduce strangers.

Step-by-Step (Do These First)

Inside Messenger:

  • ☐ Open Messenger → tap the menu/profile icon (top corner)
  • ☐ Open Privacy or Safety (names vary by version)
  • ☐ Tighten Message Requests (reduce/disable requests from strangers)
  • ☐ Tighten Calls (restrict who can call/message)
  • ☐ Turn off Active Status if you do not want people seeing when they are online
  • ☐ Review the contact list and block unknown accounts

If you can’t find a setting, use the Messenger search bar in Settings and type: privacy, message requests, or calls.

House Rules (Simple + Strong)

  • ☐ No private chats with people they do not know in real life.
  • ☐ No photos/voice notes to online-only contacts.
  • ☐ If anyone asks for secrecy or to move platforms, stop and tell a parent.
