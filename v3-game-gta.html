GTA / Mature Games

Mature titles are not child safe by default. This page helps you reduce risk.

Why GTA-style games are high risk

Mature games expose kids to adult themes, violent content, and often unfiltered online chat. Even if the game “looks fun”, open voice chat and random lobbies can introduce serious risk fast.

Safety locks (do these first)

1) Disable voice chat or set to friends-only (if available).

2) Block messages from non-friends.

3) Restrict who can join sessions / invite.

4) Keep gameplay in shared spaces (not bedroom-only).

5) Enforce age-appropriate content decisions at the platform level.

Platform-first controls (strongest layer)

Most of the real protection comes from your device/platform settings: content ratings, installs, time limits, and communication controls.

Parent rule (simple)

If the game is rated for adults, treat it like an adult space. If you wouldn’t allow the room, don’t allow the lobby.
