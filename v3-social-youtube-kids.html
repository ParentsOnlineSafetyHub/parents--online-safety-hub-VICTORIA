Social Apps › YouTube Kids

Quick win (60 seconds)

If you do nothing else today: set content to Approved content only (parent picks channels/videos).

Step-By-Step (simple clicks)

1) Open YouTube Kids (parent device recommended)
2) Go to Settings (🔒) and enter your parent passcode
3) Select your child’s profile
4) Choose content level appropriate for age
5) For maximum control: choose Approved content only
6) Turn OFF Search if you want tighter boundaries
7) Review watch history and block anything that feels wrong

Pair with device controls (Screen Time / Family Link) for the strongest setup.
