Why This Exists

Kids are growing up in a digital world most parents never experienced. Gaming platforms, messaging apps, livestreams, private chats — they move faster than most adults can keep up with.

Parents Online Safety Hub was built to close that gap. Not through fear. Not through politics. Through prevention.

Prevention is better than cure.

What This Website Provides

  • Practical 10-minute safety setups
  • Clear warning signs of online grooming
  • Victoria-based reporting pathways
  • Printable family agreements
  • Ongoing prevention checklists

No outrage. No scare tactics. Just clear steps parents can take today.

Who Built This

Built by Graeme (aka Jinglez), a Victorian parent who believes education and structure are stronger than reaction.

This is a community-driven initiative focused on empowering parents before harm occurs.

Funding & Transparency

This platform is independently built and maintained. Funding helps cover:

  • Website hosting & technical upgrades
  • Printable resource development
  • Platform-specific safety guides (Fortnite, Roblox, etc.)
  • Educational video walkthroughs for parents
  • Future expansion across Australia

Support is optional. The core resources will always remain accessible.
