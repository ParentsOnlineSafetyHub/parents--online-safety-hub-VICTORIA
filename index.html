Why This Website Exists

Children are growing up in a digital environment that evolves faster than most parents were ever taught to navigate. Online gaming, private messaging, livestream platforms and social media introduce risks that many families feel unprepared to manage.

Parents Online Safety Hub was created to provide structured, practical guidance that parents can apply immediately.

No alarmism. No politics. Just prevention.

Prevention is better than cure.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Clear 10-minute safety setup instructions
  • Recognisable warning signs of online grooming
  • Victoria-based reporting pathways
  • Printable family safety resources
  • Ongoing monthly prevention checklists

Everything is written to be simple, practical and usable by parents regardless of technical experience.

About the Initiative

Built in Victoria by Graeme (aka Jinglez), this is a community-driven education project focused on strengthening parental confidence in the digital space.

The goal is simple: equip families with knowledge before harm occurs.

Funding & Transparency

This platform is independently developed and maintained. Voluntary support helps cover:

  • Website hosting and technical upgrades
  • Printable resource development
  • Platform-specific safety guides (gaming & messaging apps)
  • Parent education walkthrough videos
  • Future expansion across Australia

Core safety resources will remain accessible regardless of funding.
