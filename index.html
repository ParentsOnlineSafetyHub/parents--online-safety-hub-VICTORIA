Parent Online Safety Checklist

Use this checklist weekly or monthly. The goal is consistency — not perfection. Small routine checks reduce risk and increase confidence.

DOWNLOAD CHECKLIST (PDF) PRINT THIS PAGE

Quick Setup (10 Minutes)

  • ☐ Accounts set to Private (where available)
  • ☐ Messaging set to Friends Only
  • ☐ Voice chat restricted/disabled for younger kids
  • ☐ Location sharing OFF
  • ☐ Unknown friend requests off / approval required
  • ☐ Devices charge overnight in a shared family area

Family Rules

  • ☐ No moving chats to new apps without parent approval
  • ☐ No sharing personal details (school, suburb, routines)
  • ☐ No sending photos to online-only contacts
  • ☐ Weekly calm check-in: “Anything weird happen online this week?”
  • ☐ Parents manage privacy/safety settings (not necessarily reading messages)

Warning Signs (Red Flags)

  • ☐ Requests for secrecy or “Don’t tell your parents”
  • ☐ Gifts, credits, skins or money offers
  • ☐ Pressure to move to private calls or other platforms
  • ☐ Requests for photos/video, “proof”, or “dares”
  • ☐ Questions about where they live, school, or routines

If You Need To Act

  • ☐ Screenshot usernames, messages, and dates/times
  • ☐ Report inside the platform/app
  • ☐ Block the account (after preserving evidence)
  • ☐ If immediate danger: call 000
  • ☐ Use official reporting pathways (Victoria Police / ACCCE / eSafety)
