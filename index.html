A National Approach To Online Safety

Children are growing up in a digital environment that moves faster than most parents were ever taught to manage. Online gaming, private messaging, livestream platforms and social media introduce risks that many families feel unprepared to navigate.

Parents Online Safety Hub was built in Victoria, with the intention of creating a clear, structured prevention model that can be applied Australia-wide.

Because co-parenting is hard — but it becomes easier when everyone is working from the same boundaries, the same rules, and the same prevention strategies.

No Alarmism. No Politics. Just Prevention.

Prevention Is Better Than Cure.

Why This Matters

When parents are aligned, children are safer. When households share consistent digital boundaries, manipulation becomes harder.

This platform is focused on practical tools — not outrage — so parents across Australia can operate from the same baseline of awareness.
