When to move faster

Some situations need calm action now, not more scrolling.

Move faster if there are threats, sexual requests, blackmail, pressure to keep secrets, requests for photos, or talk about meeting in person.

Do not shame. Do not panic. Do not delete evidence first. Slow the contact down and get help.

Platform risk pathways

The biggest risk is often not the first platform. It is where the contact moves next.

Roblox may start contact. Discord may deepen it. Snapchat may hide it. Instagram may personalise it. TikTok may expose it.

The pattern matters more than the app name.

Devices, controls, and trust

Controls reduce exposure. Monitoring keeps risk visible. Trust keeps children talking.

Controls

Use device settings, app limits, account restrictions, and safer defaults.

Visibility

Keep devices, chats, accounts, downloads, and late-night use visible.

Conversation

Make it easier for your child to tell you before something gets worse.

Why POSH exists

POSH exists because too many families only learn about online danger after harm has already started.

This platform was built to help parents act earlier, understand more, and protect with confidence instead of panic.

Child safety is the goal.
