Why POSH exists

Kids are online every day. Risks don’t announce themselves — they blend in. Most parents aren’t failing; they’re navigating fast-changing platforms with unsafe default settings.

POSH Aussie exists to make this simple: age-appropriate rules, locked settings, and calm, consistent boundaries. Not panic. Not shame. Just prevention that works.
