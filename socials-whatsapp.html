Primary Risk Areas

  • ☐ Group invites from unknown people
  • ☐ Calls/messages from unknown numbers
  • ☐ Disappearing messages
  • ☐ Sharing photos/info into large groups

Immediate Lockdown Settings

  • ☐ Groups → My Contacts (who can add them)
  • ☐ Profile Photo → Contacts Only
  • ☐ About → Contacts Only
  • ☐ Last Seen / Online → Contacts Only
  • ☐ Review group list monthly

House Rule

No joining groups unless a parent knows who runs it and who is inside it.
