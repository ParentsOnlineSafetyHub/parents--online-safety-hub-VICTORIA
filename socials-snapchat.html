Primary Risk Areas

  • ☐ Snap Map location sharing
  • ☐ Quick Add (strangers)
  • ☐ Disappearing photos/messages
  • ☐ “Streak” pressure and private chats

Immediate Lockdown Settings

  • ☐ Snap Map → Ghost Mode ON
  • ☐ Contact Me → Friends Only
  • ☐ View My Story → Friends Only (or Custom)
  • ☐ Quick Add → OFF / restricted
  • ☐ Review friends monthly

Non-Negotiable Rule

No photos to online-only contacts. No private chats with strangers.
