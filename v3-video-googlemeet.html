Why Google Meet needs supervision

Meet links can be forwarded. If a child joins meetings from unknown sources, risk increases.

Step-by-step: safer Meet calls

1) Use trusted accounts
Prefer school-managed Google accounts when available.

2) Don’t reuse meeting links
Use fresh links for private meetings (family calls, tutoring, etc.).

3) Only join meetings from known sources
School email or known parent only — never random links.

4) Control screen sharing
If screen sharing is available, restrict it for kids meetings when possible.

5) Supervise younger children
Calls in shared spaces. End meeting immediately if unknown user appears.
