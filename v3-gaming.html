Why gaming matters

  • Games are social spaces, not just entertainment
  • Children can talk to strangers through text, voice, party chat, or private servers
  • Predators often build trust through shared games before moving chats elsewhere
  • Many parents don’t realise games can be the starting point of grooming
Golden Rule: if someone asks your child to move from a game to Discord, Snapchat, Telegram, or another private app, that is a major red flag.
