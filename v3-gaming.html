Golden Rule

If someone asks your child to move chats off the game (to Discord / Snapchat / Telegram / WhatsApp / Instagram DMs) — that’s a RED FLAG.

Your child tells you immediately. No punishment for speaking up.

Predators use games to build trust → isolate → move off-platform.

High exposure games

If your child plays these, lock them first: chat, voice, friend requests, invites, and private messages.

Roblox Minecraft Fortnite GTA / Mature Games Call of Duty

Quick rules (works for every game)

1) Voice chat OFF (or friends-only).

2) Friend requests = real-life friends only.

3) Private messages OFF (or friends-only).

4) No sharing name, school, suburb, photos, or location.

5) Devices charge overnight in a shared area.

Parent script: “If anything feels weird, you’re not in trouble. Tell me and we’ll fix it together.”

Platform first (strongest protection)

Console/PC controls matter more than in-game settings. Lock installs, chat, screen time, and age ratings at the platform level.
