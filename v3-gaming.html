Why gaming safety matters

  • Games are social spaces with voice and messaging.
  • Children often talk with strangers during gameplay.
  • Predators build trust through shared games.
  • Chats may move from games to private apps.
Golden Rule: If someone asks a child to move from a game chat to Discord, Snapchat, Telegram or another app — treat that as a red flag.
