Gaming

Lock the platform first — then lock the game settings.

Lock gaming properly (3 steps)

1) Lock the console/PC profile (family controls ON).

2) Turn voice chat OFF (or friends-only).

3) Restrict friend requests and private messages.

High exposure games (start here)

These games have high social contact, chat features, or open communities. If your child plays these, lock them first.

Roblox Minecraft Fortnite GTA / Mature Games Call of Duty

Quick rules (works for every game)

1) Voice chat OFF (or friends-only).

2) Friend requests = real-life friends only.

3) Private messages OFF (or friends-only).

4) No sharing name, school, suburb, photos, or location.

5) Devices charge overnight in a shared area.
