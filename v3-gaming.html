Gaming

Lock the platform first — then lock the game settings.

Top 3 High Exposure (start here)

These show up the most because they mix large youth audiences with chat/voice, open communities, and private spaces where strangers can contact kids.

Roblox Discord VRChat

Common pattern: a stranger starts contact inside a game, then tries to move your child to private messaging (Discord / Snapchat / Telegram / WhatsApp) where supervision is weaker.

Grooming: warning signs Predators & off-platform luring

High exposure games

If your child plays these, lock them early.

Minecraft Fortnite GTA / Mature Games Call of Duty

Golden Rules (works for every game)

1) Voice chat OFF (or friends-only).

2) DMs OFF (or friends-only).

3) Friend requests = real-life friends only.

4) No sharing name, school, suburb, photos, or location.

5) If anyone asks for secrecy, photos, gifts, or to move apps → tell a parent immediately.
