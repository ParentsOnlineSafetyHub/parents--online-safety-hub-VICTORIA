Gaming

Games can be social spaces. Lock the platform first, then the game settings.

High Exposure Warning

THE GOLDEN RULE

If someone asks your child to move chats off the game (Discord / Snapchat / Telegram / WhatsApp / Instagram DMs) — that’s a RED FLAG.

Your child should tell you immediately. No punishment. No blame. Just safety.

Quick rules for every game

1) Voice chat OFF (or friends-only).

2) Friend requests = real-life friends only.

3) Private messages OFF (or friends-only).

4) No sharing name, school, suburb, photos, or location.

5) Devices charge overnight in a shared area.

Script for kids: “If anyone asks you to keep secrets or to add them on Discord / Snap / Telegram, tell me straight away.”
