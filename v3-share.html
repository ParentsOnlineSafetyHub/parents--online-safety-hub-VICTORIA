Simple share message

“This site makes online child safety clearer for parents. If you’ve felt unsure where to start, this can help you work out the next step fast.”

Stronger message if something already feels off

“If something about your child’s online use feels off, don’t ignore it. This site helps parents spot warning signs, check the biggest risks first, and know what to do next.”

Message for family or community groups

“Sharing this because a lot of parents still do not know how online grooming, private chat movement, hidden apps, and warning signs actually look in real life. This site explains it clearly.”

Message for schools, support groups, or community pages

“POSH is a practical child safety resource that helps parents understand online risks, warning signs, reporting steps, device safety, and what to do when something feels wrong.”
