Why sharing matters

Many parents only find safety information after something has already gone wrong.

Sharing POSH early can help another parent recognise risk sooner and act faster.

One share can reach a parent who needs help today
Keep it simple:
You do not need to send someone the whole site at once.
Send the page that matches what they are worried about most.

Best pages to share first

Home Start Here Safety Check Red Flags Report & Get Help
If a parent feels overwhelmed, the best pages to send first are usually Start Here, Safety Check, or Red Flags.

What page should you send?

If something feels wrong If they need action now If the issue is gaming If the issue is apps or chats
If they need words to use If they need stronger boundaries If they suspect grooming If there is blackmail or pressure

Simple share message

“This site makes online child safety clearer for parents. If you’ve felt unsure where to start, this can help you work out the next step fast.”

Stronger message if something already feels off

“If something about your child’s online use feels off, don’t ignore it. This site helps parents spot warning signs, check the biggest risks first, and know what to do next.”
