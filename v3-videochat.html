Video & Live Communication

Live chat is still chat. Lock it like any other platform.

Why this matters

Video tools feel “safe” because they’re used for school and work. But they still allow private chat, unknown joins, screen sharing, and contact requests.

Choose a platform

Skype Zoom Microsoft Teams Webex Google Meet FaceTime

Universal safety locks

1) Don’t share meeting links publicly.

2) Disable private chat where possible.

3) Host-only screen sharing.

4) Enable waiting rooms (or approvals).

5) Block unknown contacts immediately.
