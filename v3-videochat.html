Live Video & Streaming

Live contact increases risk fast. Voice, video, and live chat can escalate manipulation quickly.

Why live platforms are higher risk

Live platforms reduce pause time. Kids are responding in real time, often under pressure.

Voice and video make manipulation feel more personal, more believable, and harder to step back from.

Live contact = faster pressure + faster escalation

Main risks

  • Private voice calls with strangers
  • Pressure to turn camera on
  • Requests to move to another app
  • Live chat grooming and flattery
  • Adults mixing with children in shared spaces

What to lock down

1) Camera access only when needed

2) Voice chat OFF or friends-only

3) No private calls with strangers

4) No moving from one platform to another

5) Parent-aware use for younger children

Parent rules that work

“No private voice or video with people you do not know in real life.”
“If anyone asks you to turn your camera on, asks for privacy, or wants to move apps — tell me straight away.”
