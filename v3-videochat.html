Video & Live Communication Platforms — if it has live chat, it needs supervision.

Why video platforms matter

Video platforms feel “safe” because they look like school or work tools. But live video + private chat + screen sharing creates risk if settings are open.

Lock meetings. Disable random joins. Control chat. Supervise usage.

Video Platform Guides

Skype Zoom Microsoft Teams Cisco Webex Google Meet FaceTime

Universal Safety Rules

1) Meeting links are never shared publicly.

2) Private chat disabled unless required.

3) Screen sharing restricted to host only.

4) Waiting rooms enabled where available.

5) Unknown contacts blocked immediately.
