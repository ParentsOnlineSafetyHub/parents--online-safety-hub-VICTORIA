Video & Live Communication Platforms

Live chat + private messages + screen sharing = increased risk if not locked down.

Why these platforms matter

Video platforms feel safe because they are used for school and work. But live communication tools allow private messaging, unknown joins, screen sharing, and external contact if settings are open.

Lock meetings. Restrict chat. Supervise use.

Platform Guides

Skype Zoom Microsoft Teams Cisco Webex Google Meet FaceTime

Universal Safety Rules

1) Never share meeting links publicly.

2) Disable private chat where possible.

3) Host-only screen sharing.

4) Waiting rooms enabled.

5) Block unknown contacts immediately.
