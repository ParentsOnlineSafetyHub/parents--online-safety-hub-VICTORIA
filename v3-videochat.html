Why Video Platforms Require Supervision

Live video removes the barrier of text. It allows:

• Real-time visual contact • Screen sharing • Private meeting links • Recording • Direct 1-on-1 interaction

Video platforms are often used for school or family — but default settings may allow unwanted contact.

Universal Safety Rules

1) Never share meeting links publicly

2) Disable screen sharing for participants

3) Disable recording access

4) Use waiting rooms or approval systems

5) Supervise younger children during calls

6) Remove unknown contacts immediately
