POSH is growing as a practical child safety platform. This page helps organisations, schools, media, community groups, and supporters choose the right contact lane so conversations start with clarity.
This page is not just for formal organisations.
It is also for educators, parents, advocates, supporters, creators, and people who want to build something useful with POSH.

Why this page exists

As POSH grows, different people will reach out for different reasons.

Some want collaboration. Some want media contact. Some want to support the project. Others simply want to connect POSH with the right audience.
