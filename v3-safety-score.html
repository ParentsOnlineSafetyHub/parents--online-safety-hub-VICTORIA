Safety Check

10 checks. Clear score. Clear next steps.

Fast Risk Scan

This takes about 60 seconds.

Be honest — the goal is not perfection, it’s reducing risk quickly.

A few quick fixes can dramatically reduce child exposure

Answer these 10 questions

1) Device controls are ON (Screen Time / Family Link / Family Safety)
2) App installs are restricted
3) DMs/private messages are OFF or friends-only
4) Voice chat is OFF or friends-only
5) Friends list is real-life people only (parent-approved)
6) No secret accounts
7) Devices charge overnight in a shared area
8) Location sharing is OFF
9) Your child knows: “You won’t be in trouble for telling me”
10) The off-platform rule is clear (no moving to Discord/Snap/Telegram with strangers)
