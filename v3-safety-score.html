Golden Rule

If someone asks your child to move chats off the game/app (Discord / Snapchat / Telegram / WhatsApp / Instagram DMs) — that’s a RED FLAG.

Your child should tell you immediately. No punishment. No blame. Just safety.

Move chat off-platform = grooming escalation risk

Answer these (takes 60 seconds)

Be honest — this is not about judgment. It’s about reducing risk fast.

1) Device controls are ON (Screen Time / Family Link / Family Safety)
2) App installs are restricted (child can’t freely install anything)
3) DMs / private messages are OFF or friends-only on main apps/games
4) Voice chat is OFF or friends-only (games + Discord/VR apps)
5) Friends list is real-life people only (parent-approved)
6) No secret accounts (parents can check anytime)
7) Devices charge overnight in a shared area (not bedrooms)
8) Location sharing is OFF (unless truly needed)
9) Your child knows: “You won’t be in trouble for telling me”
10) Off-platform rule is clear (no moving to Discord/Snap/Telegram)
